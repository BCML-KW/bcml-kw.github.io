Hyunsoo Yu is currently a researcher at LG Innotek. He received his BS degree in Robotics Engineering from Kwangwoon University in Seoul, South Korea.

His research interests include experiment setting, signal processing, machine learning and artificial intelligence, with a focus on sleep staging and biomedical signal analysis. He was responsible for managing all experimental devices in the lab.
